Guess The Footballer Game

Rules & How to Play


1. Please register or login to begin playing.

2. An image of a footballer will appear.

3. Enter the surname only of the player in the field provided and click submit.

4. You will only get one attempt for each question.

5. If the surname is mis-spelt this will be deemed an incorrect answer

6. Each correct answer will score one point

7. There will be a total of 20 questions

8. Once you have completed all 20 questions, check the scoreboard to see where you rank!
